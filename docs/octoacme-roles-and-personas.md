# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers focus on usability and customer experience, translating business requirements into user flows and interface designs that optimize the product for end users.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Work with Product Managers to align design with user needs
- Collaborate with Developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation fidelity
- Maintain design systems and accessibility standards

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, user-friendly interfaces
- Minimize friction and improve user satisfaction
- Ensure consistency across product experiences

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews during sprint planning and weekly syncs
- Collaboration with Product Managers on user stories
- Handoff sessions with Developers for implementation details
- Usability testing sessions with QA and stakeholders

### Interaction notes
UX Designers partner closely with **Product Managers** to understand user needs and business goals, with **Developers** to ensure designs are technically feasible and properly implemented, and with **QA** to validate usability through testing. They present design proposals to **Stakeholders** for feedback and work with the **Project Manager** to ensure design milestones align with delivery schedules.

---

## Tech Lead / Architect

### Role Summary
Tech Leads or Architects oversee technical solution design, ensure code and system quality, and provide technical mentorship to the development team.

### Responsibilities
- Design system architecture and technical solutions
- Review code and enforce coding standards
- Resolve complex technical issues and performance bottlenecks
- Mentor developers and facilitate technical discussions
- Evaluate and recommend technologies and tools
- Ensure security, scalability, and maintainability of solutions

### Goals
- Deliver robust, scalable technical solutions
- Maintain high code quality and system reliability
- Foster technical growth within the team

### Typical Communication
- Architecture syncs and technical design reviews
- Code review feedback and technical guidance
- Participation in sprint planning for technical estimation
- Documentation of architectural decisions and patterns

### Interaction notes
Tech Leads collaborate with **Project Managers** on delivery timelines and technical risk assessment, with **Product Managers** to evaluate solution trade-offs and feasibility, with **Developers** for implementation guidance and code reviews, and with **QA** to ensure testability of solutions. They also advise **Stakeholders** on technical constraints and opportunities.

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts clarify requirements, map business processes, and support the definition of acceptance criteria to bridge the gap between business needs and technical delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and analyze business requirements from stakeholders
- Document detailed requirement specifications and user stories
- Map business workflows and identify process improvements
- Collaborate with Product Managers on prioritization and scope
- Validate that delivered solutions meet business needs
- Facilitate communication between business and technical teams

### Goals
- Ensure clear, complete requirements with minimal ambiguity
- Reduce rework through accurate requirement gathering
- Align technical solutions with business objectives

### Typical Communication
- Requirement gathering sessions with stakeholders
- Story refinement workshops with Product Managers and team
- Acceptance criteria reviews and validation sessions
- Documentation of business rules and process flows

### Interaction notes
Business Analysts work closely with **Product Managers** to translate vision into detailed requirements, with **Project Managers** to manage scope and timelines, with **Developers** to clarify technical questions about requirements, with **QA** to define acceptance criteria and test cases, and with **Stakeholders** to elicit needs and validate solutions.

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers own the release process, coordinate staging and production deployments, and ensure smooth, reliable delivery to production environments.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain release calendar and schedule deployment windows
- Verify release readiness and coordinate with stakeholders
- Oversee staging and production deployments
- Communicate release status and deployment schedules
- Manage rollbacks and incident response during releases
- Track release metrics and identify process improvements

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, low-risk releases to production
- Minimize deployment-related incidents and downtime
- Maintain clear communication around release schedules

### Typical Communication
- Release planning meetings with development and QA teams
- Status updates to stakeholders before and after deployments
- Coordination via release calendar and deployment notifications
- Post-release retrospectives to improve process

### Interaction notes
Release Managers coordinate with **Developers** and **QA** to verify release readiness, with **Project Managers** to align releases with project milestones, with **Product Managers** on feature priorities and release scope, and with **Stakeholders** for deployment announcements and change communication. They also work with the **Tech Lead** to ensure technical deployment requirements are met.
